Evil Crow Cable project

Evil Crow Cable

During the year, I purchased an Evil Crow Cable through AliExpress. I had never heard about this cable before, but upon reading about it, I felt it would be a good purchase to help educate myself on more modern methods of security attack vectors.

What did I discover/learn

The Evil Crow Cable is a USB cable, the same as any other, but contained within the plastic shielding is an Attiny85 micro-controller. This controller has the ability to store and execute scripts on remote computers. This was my first hands-on experience with a pen-testing micro-controller.

During my time spent using the Evil Crow Cable, I learnt all about the different attacks that can be performed simply from a USB port. They can be used for malicious activities such as keylogger attacks, file deletion, website defacing, or simple pranks like printing text to notepad.

It was a big eye-opener as I wasn't aware such devices existed, and it made me much more aware of the need for education about security threats and best practices when it comes to security threats.

I have created a short presentation about my experience with the Evil Crow Cable. I am yet to present this at the time of writing this portfolio section, but I intend to use the presentation to make others aware that such products exist, what they are capable of and how to help prevent attacks from these products.
